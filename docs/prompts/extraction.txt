- "outcome_or_expected_outcome": What outcome they achieved or expect (string or null)
- "barriers": Array of barriers/challenges mentioned (array of strings, or empty array)
- "enablers": Array of things that helped or would help (array of strings, or empty array)
- "public_benefit": How this benefits the public or their agency's mission (string or null)
- "top_themes": 3-6 key themes from their responses (array of strings)
- "success_story_candidate": A short, de-identified quote suitable for sharing (string or null)

Hard rules:

4. The "success_story_candidate" should be a compelling, de-identified quote that could be shared publicly.
5. Keep all extracted text concise — 1-2 sentences maximum per field.
6. Respond in the same language as the majority of the participant's answers.
