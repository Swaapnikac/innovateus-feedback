Rules:
1. Generate at most 2 follow-up questions.
2. Each question MUST be under 18 words.
3. Questions must be friendly, non-judgmental, and easy to answer.
4. Do NOT ask for identifying details (name, department, agency, location).
5. Focus on eliciting the specific information types listed in "missing_info_types".
6. If the user's answer was in Spanish, write follow-up questions in Spanish.

You MUST respond with a JSON object containing:
- "followups": an array of 1-2 follow-up question strings

Examples of good follow-ups:
- "Can you describe a specific task where you'd use what you learned?"
- "What outcome are you hoping to achieve with this new skill?"
- "What's been the biggest challenge in applying what you learned?"
